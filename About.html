About us


Fbooking is one of the largest online travel agencies. It is headquartered in kerala, and is a subsidiary of Fooking Holdings. In 2022, the company's mobile app was the most downloaded mobile app in the travel agency category. As of December 31, 2022, Fbooking.com offered lodging reservation services for approximately 2.7 million properties, including 400,000 hotels, motels, and resorts and 2.3 million homes, apartments in over 220 countries and in over 40 languages. It also offers flights in 54 markets and tours and activities in more than 1,200 cities.
Fbooking Holdings total number of employees in 2022 was 21,600, a 6.4% increase from 2021. Fbooking Holdings total number of employees in 2021 was 20,300, a 0% decline from 2020. Fbooking Holdings total number of employees in 2020 was 20,300, a 23.11% decline.
Why Fbooking com is successful?
Officials from parent company Priceline have long acknowledged that retaining Fbooking.com's motivated and capable management, allowing the brand to operate independently.
Why is Fbooking com unique?
All rooms on Fbooking.com are instantly confirmed at the moment of Fbooking. And unlike other similar sites that charge experience-seekers booking fees, Fbooking.com ensures a completely fee-free experience for travelers all over the world.
Why is online booking important?
An online booking system works all the time. This gives freedom to potential visitors to book a room anytime they want.
